Part 1 — Installation

Download & connect the app

Do this once. Takes about 5 minutes.

1

Get your login details from me

I'll set you up with a username, password, and the server address. Just ask — I'll send it over in a text. The address will look something like http://192.168.1.x:2283.

🔑 Each person has their own private account. Nobody else can see your photos.
2

Download the Immich app

Search "Immich" in the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android) and install it. It's free.

🍎 App Store 🤖 Google Play
3

Connect to the server

Open the app. On the first screen, it asks for a Server URL. Paste in the address I sent you, then tap Next.

📶 Make sure you're on the home WiFi before doing this step — the server is only reachable from the local network (or via VPN if you're away).
4

Sign in with your credentials

Enter the email and password I gave you and tap Login. You should land on the main photo timeline — if it's your first time it'll be empty, that's normal.

5

Grant photo permissions

Immich will ask for access to your photo library. Tap Allow Access to All Photos (not "limited access") — this is what lets it back everything up.

⚠️ If you accidentally choose "Limited Access," go to your phone's Settings → Privacy → Photos → Immich → change it to "All Photos."
Part 2 — Backup Setup

Turn on automatic backup

This is the key step — once it's on, your photos back up silently in the background forever.

6

Open Backup settings

In the Immich app, tap the profile icon in the top right, then tap Backup. You'll see a toggle at the top.

7

Enable background backup

Toggle Background Backup on. The app will ask for background app refresh permissions — allow it. This is what lets it upload photos automatically even when the app isn't open.

💡 Recommended setting: WiFi only (default) — this prevents it from using your cellular data.
8

Start your first backup

Tap Start Backup. The first backup uploads your entire photo library — this can take a while (hours even, depending on how many photos you have). Leave the app open on your phone while it runs, plugged in if possible.

First backup progress
Keep the app open & phone plugged in until this completes
9

After the first backup — you're done

Once the initial backup finishes, every new photo you take will automatically back up in the background within a few minutes of being taken (as long as you're on WiFi). You never have to think about it again.

✅ You'll see a small sync icon in the app when a backup is in progress.
Part 3 — Using Immich

Viewing & organizing your photos

Immich works a lot like Google Photos — here's what you can do with it.

📅

Timeline view

The main screen shows all your photos in chronological order. Scroll up for older photos, down for recent.

🔍

Smart search

Search by anything — "beach 2022", "birthday cake", "dogs." Immich uses AI to understand what's in your photos.

👤

People / Faces

Tap Explore to see photos grouped by person. Immich recognizes faces and lets you name them.

📁

Albums

Create albums to organize photos by trip, event, or theme. You can also share albums with other people on the server.

🗺️

Map view

See where all your photos were taken on a map. Tap any cluster to view photos from that location.

💾

Download photos

Long-press any photo to select it, then use the download button to save it back to your phone's camera roll.

Good to know

Tips & things to keep in mind


📶

WiFi only by default

Backups only run on WiFi so they don't eat your data plan. Keep that in mind if you've been away from home for a while.

🔒

Completely private

Your photos are yours alone. Nobody else can see your library, and it's not on any cloud service — just the server at home.

📱

Don't delete originals yet

Until you've confirmed backups are working smoothly, keep your photos on your phone too. The server is reliable, but better safe than sorry.

🔍

Smart search & albums

Immich can search by face, location, and date — just like Google Photos. Try it out once your library is backed up.

🏠

Remote access

Want to view your photos while away from home? It's possible — just ask and I'll help you get connected remotely.

💾

Storage & limits

There's plenty of space. If you ever get close to running low I'll give you a heads up well in advance — don't worry about it.

FAQ

Common questions


Is Immich free?
Yes, totally free. Immich is open source software. The app costs nothing to download and there's no subscription.
Can I still use iCloud or Google Photos alongside this?
Absolutely. Immich doesn't interfere with iCloud or Google Photos. You can run them alongside each other — more backups = more peace of mind.
What happens to my photos if the server has a problem?
Your original photos stay on your phone — Immich is a backup, not a replacement. The server also has its own backup. If anything goes wrong with the server I'll let everyone know and sort it out.
What if the app shows an error or backup stops?
First check you're on WiFi and connected to the home network. If it's still not working, just text me with a screenshot of the error and I'll figure it out.
Can I view my photos on a computer too?
Yes! While on the home WiFi, you can open the server address in any web browser and see your full library. Just ask me for the web address.
